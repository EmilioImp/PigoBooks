FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

"I forgot the password for my account and therefore I can't access it anymore! How can I solve that problem?"

Reach us compiling the form in "Contact Us" and we will make sure to help you regaining access to your account!

"What kind of books can I buy using this website?"

You can see the kind of books available by checking the navigation bar: you can use the "Genres"/"Themes" link according to your preferences, or if you are interested in checking if we sell books written by your favourite author you can use the "Authors" link. You can also use the "Best Sellers" link to check the most loved books during this month, and the "Our Favourites" link if you are curious about what our team likes the most.

"How do I have to behave if the book I ordered on this website doesn't arrive in the estimated time?"

You can use the info in the "Contact Us" link to reach our team and we will try to solve the situation as quickly and comfortably as possible for you.

"I really love [author's name] and I would really like to attend his/her latest book's presentation: where can I see if he/she has planned some sort of event (and eventually take notes about the info regarding it)?"

We have a dedicated link in our navigation tab about that topic: if you press on "Events" you'll have the possibility to check all the programmed events regarding latest books' presentation (and the relative informations) and you can even search if there are any planned events for that book you long for.

"I have wrongly ordered a book I changed my mind about and now I don't know what to do!"

Text us using the "Contact Us" page and we will get in touch with you to gather further information and value a cancellation of that order.

"Where can I have information about the previous orders I have made with my account?"

You can see your transation history in the page "Order History", accessible from your "Profile" page, the "Account" card or through the "Shopping Cart" page.
