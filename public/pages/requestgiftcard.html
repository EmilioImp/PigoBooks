Requesting a Gift Card

Redemption

Gift cards can be used on the Pigo Books website. The value of any purchases made using the gift card will be deducted from the current balance on the gift card. Any remaining balance will remain in the gift card recipient's account. If an order exceeds the amount of the gift card, the balance must be paid with a credit card.

Limitations

There is no limit on the number of gift cards that can be purchased, but the balance of any particular gift card may not exceed $100. Gift cards cannot be reloaded, resold, transferred for value, redeemed for cash or applied to any other account, except to the extent required by law. Unused gift card balances in Pigo Books account may not be transferred.

Risk of loss

The risk of loss and title for gift cards pass to the purchaser upon our electronic transmission to the recipient. We are not responsible for lost or stolen gift cards.

Fraud

Pigo Books will have the right to close customer accounts and request alternative forms of payment if a fraudulently obtained gift card is either redeemed or used to make purchases on the website.
